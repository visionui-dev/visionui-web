Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

At VisionUI, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit visionui.app and use our services.

analytics
1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email, name (optional), and encrypted password when creating an account.
  • Purchase information: Email and transaction data processed through LemonSqueezy.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, and actions taken on the site.
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device used.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your experience. See the Cookies section below.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

Service Delivery

Subscription management, technical support, and software license authentication.

Internal Optimization

Analysis to identify bugs, optimize performance, and prioritize new features.

Communications

Sending emails related to your account, purchases, and service updates.

Security

Detection and prevention of fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to our systems.

Our site uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. The types we use:

Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies to show ads based on previous visits to this or other sites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Payment processors: LemonSqueezy to process transactions.
  • Email services: Resend to send transactional emails.
  • Advertising services: Google AdSense to display ads.
  • Hosting: Cloudflare and GitHub to host our services.
  • Legal authorities: When required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Password encryption with secure algorithms (bcrypt)
  • HTTPS connections across the entire site
  • Restricted access to personal data
  • Regular vulnerability monitoring

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request portability of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@visionui.app.

Our services are not directed at users under 16 years of age. We do not intentionally collect information from minors. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.
